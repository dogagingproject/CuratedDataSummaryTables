About the Environment Survey

The Environment Survey focuses on the dog’s physical and built environment, chemical environment and exposures, and social environment. In Terra, these owner-reported values are paired with zip code-specific environment data, including census tract-level sociodemographic and economic indicators, air pollution indicators, climate and weather variables, and walkability scores. Participant address, including zip code, are withheld from Curated Data releases to protect privacy.

Survey Instrument: HLES Environment
