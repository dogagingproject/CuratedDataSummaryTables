About the Medications and Preventives Survey

The Medications and Preventives Survey collects data on dental care, grooming, preventive treatments, vaccination and supplements. Additional data related to diet-based supplementation is available in the Diet and Comprehensive Diet Surveys. Information about specific medical conditions is collected in the Health Status Survey.

Survey Instrument: HLES Medications and Preventives
