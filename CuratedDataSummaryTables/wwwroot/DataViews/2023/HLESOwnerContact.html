About the Owner Contact Survey

The Owner Contact Survey includes personally identifiable information (PII), which is withheld from Curated Data releases. Address information for primary residential address is reported at the level of Census Division. Participant addresses, including zip code, are withheld from Curated Data to protect privacy. All scientific studies that require geographic linkage must be done in collaboration with the DAP Data and Analysis team.
