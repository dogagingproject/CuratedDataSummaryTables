About the Environment Survey

The Environment Survey focuses on the dog’s physical and built environment, chemical environment and exposures, and social environment. In Curated Data, these owner-reported values are paired with zip code-specific environment data, including census tract-level sociodemographic and economic indicators, air pollution indicators, climate and weather variables, and walkability scores. Participant addresses, including zip code, are withheld from Curated Data to protect privacy. All scientific studies that require geographic linkage must be done in collaboration with the DAP Data and Analysis team.
